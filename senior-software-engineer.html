About Oct7

Oct7 is building the technology backbone for Jewish advocacy and community coordination. Our platform connects intelligence gathering, campaign coordination, and community mobilization into a unified system that empowers advocates worldwide. We combine cutting-edge AI with robust engineering to fight antisemitism through coordinated action.

  • Collaborate directly with product and operations teams
  • Improve our DevOps practices, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment processes
  • Participate in architecture decisions and code reviews
